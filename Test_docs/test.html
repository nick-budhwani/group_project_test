About us

Urban Chef is designed to connect busy professionals with a restaurant quality dining experience in the comfort of their own home. Our chefs offer a wide selection of their premium dishes which are delivered weekly to Urban Chef clients. All you have to do is choose a Chef from one listed in your area and choose your delicious meals for the week. Pricing is competitive with local takeout options but without the need to leave your home to enjoy your meal.

How to use the site

1. Click on sign up 2. Create an account 3. Based on location specified the site will show you the chefs catering to your area 4. Browse the chefs and view their menu selections 5. Once you found the chef for you click place order 6. From the list provided choose at least 6 dishes for your first order 7. You can view your order status anytime you return to the site and an email will be sent to you with your order details.
